Pizza

The world's greatest pizza recipe

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven. Learn the tricks for making and grilling pizza from scratch with this recipe. Feel free to use your favorite toppings, but keep in mind not to overload the pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup warm water
  • Active dry yeast
  • Pinch of white sugar
  • All purpose flour
  • Olice oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Garlic
  • Fresh basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingrediants.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Mix in flour.
  4. Knead until smooth.
  5. Cover in bowl with damp cloth.
  6. Set aside to rise.
  7. Make pizza.
  8. Place pizza on hot grill and cook.
  9. Allow to cool before serving.
