Burger

The perfect basic burger

These burger patties are made with ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm summer evening. Pile these burgers with your favorite condiments and pop open a cool drink!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Half cup dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Pre-heat grill.
  2. Whisk egg, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Add in beef and dry bread crumbs.
  4. Form in to 4 patties.
  5. Place patties on pre-heated grill.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.
